# Luxury Fashion & Apparel Intelligence Dashboard

## Overview
This project features a sophisticated **Luxury Fashion & Apparel Intelligence Dashboard** developed in **Power BI**. It is designed to provide high-level executive oversight and granular data insights for the luxury retail sector, managing a **Total GMV of €11M**. The dashboard tracks performance across top global fashion houses, omnichannel sales funnels, and high-net-worth client behaviors.

## Key Features & Insights
* **Executive Performance Tracking:** Monitors critical business health indicators including an **Average Order Value (AOV) of €9.55K** and an impressive **Repeat Client Rate of 89.33%**.
* **Client Intelligence & Tiers:** Segments customers into VIC (Very Important Client), HNW, and Standard tiers to analyze **Life Time Value (LTV)** and purchase frequency.
* **Product & Brand Performance:** Provides a detailed breakdown of revenue by brand, highlighting **Valentino (€59K)**, **Louis Vuitton (€24K)**, and **Prada (€20K)** as top performers.
* **Omnichannel Analytics:** Analyzes the sales channel mix, with **Boutiques** accounting for **53.27%** of revenue, followed by **E-Commerce (31.2%)** and **Wholesale (10.12%)**.
* **Inventory & Margin Health:** Tracks **Sell-Through Rates** and margins by category, noting that **Fine Jewellery** maintains the highest gross margin at **78.01%**.
* **Prestige Pricing:** Features a **Prestige Price Index of 389.13** to measure brand equity and pricing power within the luxury market.

## Technical Components
* **Business Intelligence Tool:** Power BI.
* **Data Visualizations:** * **Funnel Charts:** Digital purchase journeys from sessions to completed purchases.
    * **Geographic Maps:** Revenue distribution by global markets across Europe, Asia, and North America.
    * **Waterfall Charts:** Net revenue analysis after returns.
    * **Bubble Charts:** Comparison of sales channels versus AOV.
* **Key Metrics (DAX):** Implementation of specialized luxury KPIs such as **Fashion Week Uplift**, **LTD Edition Premium**, and **VIC Revenue Share**.

## Report Structure
* **Executive Overview:** High-level summary of GMV, returns, and total equity.
* **Client Intelligence:** Deep dive into top 20 VIC clients and churn risk assessment.
* **Product Performance:** Comparison of actual revenue versus targets by brand and category.
* **Omnichannel Funnel:** Analysis of the digital conversion rate (7.55%) and channel-specific AOVs.
* **Seasonal Trends:** Identification of peak month GMV and rolling revenue trends throughout the fiscal year.
